# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 2026*

## Our Commitment

Integritas Civica respects your privacy. We believe in transparency about data practices, just as we believe in transparency in public discourse.

## What We Collect

### Website Analytics

We use privacy-respecting analytics that do not use cookies, do not track you across websites, and do not collect personal information. We collect only aggregate data about page views and referral sources to understand how people find and use our site.

### Newsletter

If you subscribe to our Substack newsletter, Substack collects your email address to deliver the newsletter. Please review [Substack's privacy policy](https://substack.com/privacy) for details on how they handle subscriber data.

### Contact

If you contact us via email, we retain your message and email address only for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

## What We Don't Do

- We do not sell or share personal data with third parties
- We do not use tracking cookies
- We do not build advertising profiles
- We do not use behavioral targeting

## Third-Party Services

Our website links to third-party platforms (YouTube, Substack, social media networks). Each platform has its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

## Changes

We may update this policy as our practices evolve. Material changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page.

## Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to [contact@integritascivica.org](mailto:contact@integritascivica.org).
